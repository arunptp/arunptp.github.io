# The AI Stack – A Model Driven Architecture

> <div align="justify">As digital transformation takes hold, a new generation of applications that depend on a distributed data stack is going into production, such as the Internet of Things (IoT), customer analytics, machine learning, and fraud prevention. These apps require a radically new approach to deal with much higher data volumes and real-time data pipelines. This article is inspired from the book The Digital Transformation by  Thomas M Siebel. </div>
